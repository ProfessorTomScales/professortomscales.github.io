How to Write Discussion Posts

In short:

For doing discussion posting, I am looking for more than a simple "I agree" or a 1-2 sentence reply.
The point of the topics is to get you to think deeper about the issues and how they are impacting your life.
I am looking for a well-thought-out paragraph response.


General Guidelines:

  1. Do not assume you know what the topic's subject is about - Read about it first.
  2. Use proper grammar and spelling.


Critical Thinking Tips:

As a general guideline for these (or any critical thinking you do) think about these when writing your response:

  1. Clarity – are you making your point?
  2. Accuracy – is what you are saying actually true?
  3. Precision – are you being too vague?
  4. Relevance – is what you are saying truly related to the topic?
  5. Depth – are you going into any detail?
  6. Breadth – have you considered multiple viewpoints?
  7. Logic – does your argument/writing make sense?
