Course Objectives/Learning Outcomes

The student should be able to:

  1. identify the different mobile Operating Systems and their fundamental methods of completing tasks.
  2. Identify and use apps for person-to-person and group communication.
  3. Identify and use apps for file management.
  4. Identify and use apps for IT administration.
  5. Identify and use apps for media creation and consumption.
  6. Identify and use apps for small-business commerce functionality.
  7. Identify and use apps for customer-facing retail.
  8. Identify and use apps for Customer Relations Management.
  9. Identify and use apps for document collaboration and file management.
  10. Identify and use apps for employee and project management.
  11. Identify and use apps for research.
  12. Identify and use apps for localized advertising.
  13. Identify and impliment mobile marketing straegies.
  14. Identify and use different hardware accessories for tasks such as commerce, networking, media creation, advertising, and information-sharing.
  15. Identify methods to repurpose old hardware.
  16. Critically evaluate mobile apps for strengths and weaknesses.
