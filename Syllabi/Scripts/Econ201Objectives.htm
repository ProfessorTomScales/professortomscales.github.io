Course Objectives/Learning Outcomes

The student should be able to:

  1. evaluate decision options using marginal analysis.
  2. identify the basic economic model of supply and demand curves, and equilibrium points.
  3. explain the relationship bwtween inflation and unemployment, and thier impact on the economy.
  4. identify and calculate different measures of economic activity.
  5. identify the 4 stages of the Business Cycle, and the economic events of each stage.
  6. explain the components of each economic market, and the factors that influence change.
  7. recognize common economic policy issues and the impact of these policies.
  8. explain the gains and losses that come from international trade.
