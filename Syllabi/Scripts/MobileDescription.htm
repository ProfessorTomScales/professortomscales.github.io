Course Description

In this class students will explore the different types of mobile computing hardware and platofrms that are available, and how they can be used to enhance the productivity of employees and the capabilities of a small business. We will explore the different marketing strategies that can be used in the realm of mobile computing as a creative means to reach and serve more customers. Although there are several mobile platforms available, we will focus primarily on Apple's iOS given it's market share, and on Android given its versatility & affordability. We will also evaluate apps in several different categories for examples of good and bad app design, functionality, and customer relations.
