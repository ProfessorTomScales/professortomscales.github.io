Course Objectives/Learning Outcomes

The student should be able to:

  1. identify the basic economic model of supply and demand curves, and equilibrium points.
  2. define Utility and its impact on consumer choice.
  3. explain the 3 types of product elasticities.
  4. identify and calculate different methods and measures of production.
  5. describe resource utilization and pricing.
  6. compare and contrast the 4 industry types, and the strategies needed for a business in each.
  7. identify the decision rules for profit maximization.
  8. differentiate between welfare and social insurance programs and their objectives.
  9. define transfer payments, give examples, and describe their impact on the economy.
  10. describe the determinants of labor supply and labor demand.
